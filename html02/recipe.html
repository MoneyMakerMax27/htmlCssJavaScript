Grandma's Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

Flour: 2 Cups

Eggs: 3 Large

Butter: 1 Cup (Soft)

Brown sugar: 2 Cups

Chocolate chips: 2 Cups

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Cream the softened butter and brown sugar together in a large bowl or stand mixer until light and fluffy about 3-5 minutes.

Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.

Stir in the flour until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.

Fold in the chocolate chips.

Scoop 3-tablespoon mounds of dough onto the prepared baking sheet.

Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are lightly browned and the centers are set.

Cool on the sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
